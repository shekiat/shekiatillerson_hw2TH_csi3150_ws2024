My Favorite Book

Description:

This book has an interesting concept of a good character turning evil. The book follows Rin, the main character, as she goes from student to soldier. Rin was born an orphan, and came from a very poor district in her country. When she got accepted into her dream school, she was overjoyed to finally leave her old life behind. However, her school life was soon stopped to a halt when war had begun in her country. Setting her studies aside, Rin had became a soldier and would need to fight many gruesome battles to come. In order to protect her friends and her country, she is willing to do whatever it takes and destroy anything that gets in her way.
